About these scenarios

What is a scenario?

A scenario is a plausible future that is created to help companies explore and understand how they might need to change in order to meet the needs and conditions of the future.

How were these scenarios created?

The four IAG Climate Change Scenarios were created using the Oxford Scenario Planning approach, by IAG’s Design Centre with the support of 70 IAG EGMs, EMs and external partners. All four scenarios are futures set in 2030 in a 2 degree Celsius climate change Australia.

What is the purpose of these scenarios?

To drive strategic conversations that deliver actions against product, pricing and culture in FY21.

Help IAG make the world a safer place.

To identify changes required to enable rapid actions that reduce IAG’s material risk in light of Climate Change.
